BNIX Webmail

BNIX Webmail is a browser-based email client that lets users connect their Gmail account with Google OAuth to read, organize, and send email from a secure web interface.

Purpose of the application

The application provides mailbox access for users who choose to sign in with Google. It uses Gmail access only to deliver email features requested by the user.

  • Read mailbox folders and message content.
  • Send email and save sent messages.
  • Manage mailbox actions such as read/unread, archive, delete, star, and labels.
  • Display attachments and message metadata inside the webmail interface.

Google user data use

BNIX Webmail does not sell Google user data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties except where required to provide the email service selected by the user.
